IEEE LaunchPad Kerala, a collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, is a premier job fair aiming to connect talented individuals with innovative companies in Kerala's technical and engineering sectors. The event includes technical challenges and online tests to assess candidate's skills. Selected candidates can choose interview locations in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, easing the hiring process. It offers access to a diverse talent pool from across Kerala for companies.
